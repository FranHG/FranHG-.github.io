Second post: My Travel to Galicia

25/8/2017

I made this trip after having loved Asturias, so I wanted to continue visiting the north and I decided on Galicia, known for its sea and seafood. The places I visited were:

  1. Santiago de Compostela:The city is known for its historical and religious centre, and its main monument is the Cathedral, which is visited by millions of pilgrims.
  2. Vigo: A city with a large port for both fishing and sport, with a great quality in its seafood.
  3. Pontevedra:A city known for its estuary, considered the best of the lower estuaries, it is also a fishing town.

Third post: My Travel to Madrid

15/7/2018

In this travel I go to Madrid to know all the Beautiful places and monuments of the capital of my country, Spain. In this travel I visited:

  1. The Royal Palace: Palace located in the center of MAdrid, which is owned by the Royal Family and is also considered a Site of Cultural Interest Historical heritage of Spain.
  2. The Fountain of Cibeles: Monument and fountain in honor of the goddess cibeles, used by the team rela madrid when winning titles.
  3. Puerta de Alcalá: Monumento situado en el centro de Madrid, que anteriorente fue la puerta de la muralla de ciudad.
  4. Park "El Retiro": Park located in the center of Madrid where there are a lot of palaces and monuments from different eras.
  5. Santiago Bernabeu Stadium: Estadio situado en el centro de la ciudad, que pertenece al equip de futbol Real Madrid.

Fourth post: My Travel to Italy

15/9/2019

The last trip I made was to Italy, where I visited its most beautiful cities:

  1. Venice: City full of water, since it is composed by diverse water channels, thanks to this it is visited by millions of people.
  2. Pizza: This city only has three especially well-known monuments that can be visited, a tower, a church and a cemetery, but they are located next door and are very beautiful to look at.
  3. Florence: A city with a beautiful historic centre full of palaces and museums with works of art..
  4. Rome: Capital of Italy, full of monuments from the different periods of splendour of the Roman Empire, with a great tourist attraction and much to visit.
  5. The Vatican: is the biggest work done by the Christian Church where I could find many works of art and a big dome in its church.
