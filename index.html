First post: My Travel to Asturias

If you want to know in more detail the aspects and monuments of Asturias(click here)

My trip to Asturias was my first trip so to speak, and I have great memories of it because of the large green meadows and mountains, due to the large amount of vegetation in them and the different animals that live there, so I was able to enjoy nature in all its fullness.

In addition to this, we can also count on large ports where we can admire the sea and great historical monuments, which are found in each of its cities.

Second post: My Travel to Galicia

As I love the north of Spain, I decided to continue travelling there and reach Galicia, where although everything is green as in my previous destination, they are totally different, as they have a different essence, although they do share the great monuments, sea views, the green of their landscapes.

Besides this, this great place has a great gastronomy, from its seafood to its meat.
